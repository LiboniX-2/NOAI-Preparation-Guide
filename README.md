# 📖 About

This repository contains practical notebooks covering theoretical and applied Artificial Intelligence topics commonly tested in NOAI and relevant for IOAI preparation.

The objective is simple:

* Help future NOAI participants
* Provide structured preparation material
* Share practical implementations
* Bridge theory and application
* Promote AI Olympiad culture in Nepal

> Disclaimer: These materials reflect my personal preparation journey and understanding of NOAI-related topics. They are also the official resources from NOAI organizers.
